Meet the Developer

Hi, I'm Max

I'm a 13 year-old hobbyist vibe-coder who builds cool stuff for fun. This is my app called Faces - the app that gives your AI a face.

It has eyes that can track objects using YOLO-11, image reading and detection powered by OpenAI's GPT-4.1 or Google Gemma, and full support for multiple AI text-to-speech models.
